Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about buying ebooks, selling digital products, payments and account safety.

How do I buy an ebook?
Browse approved ebooks from homepage. Click Buy Ebook and complete payment. Purchased ebook automatically appears inside your Library.
How can I sell ebooks?
Apply using Become Seller option inside Dashboard. After admin approval you can upload ebooks.
How much earnings seller receive?
Seller receives 80% earnings. CreatorHub keeps 20% admin commission for platform maintenance.
Is refund available?
No refunds are available because all products are digital downloads. Users must verify product details before purchase.
How withdraw earnings?
Open Wallet section. Submit withdraw request. Admin approval usually takes upto 24 hours.
Why seller approval required?
Seller verification prevents spam and protects buyers from fake ebooks.
Is CreatorHub safe?
Yes. Every ebook goes through admin approval before publishing.
Can admin ban users?
Yes. Policy violation or fraud activities may result in permanent account ban.
How contact support?
Visit Contact page or Support section available inside footer menu.
