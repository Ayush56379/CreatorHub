About CreatorHub

Who We Are

CreatorHub is a modern digital ebook marketplace designed to empower creators and learners worldwide. Our platform allows authors, educators, and digital creators to upload and sell their ebooks directly to customers without middlemen.

We focus on simplicity, transparency, and fair revenue sharing so that creators can monetize their knowledge efficiently.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a trusted ecosystem where creators can earn fairly while buyers receive high-quality digital products.

  • Empower independent creators
  • Provide secure digital transactions
  • Ensure transparent revenue sharing
  • Support learning through digital resources

Revenue Model

CreatorHub operates on a fair commission model:

  • 20% platform commission per successful sale
  • 80% earnings credited directly to the seller wallet
  • No hidden fees
  • Transparent wallet tracking system

Platform Features

  • Instant digital delivery
  • Seller wallet system
  • Secure data storage
  • Search & discovery tools
  • Verified seller badge system
  • Mobile & desktop responsive design

Security & Trust

We prioritize user data protection and secure transactions. All digital purchases are handled through structured platform logic, and user information is never sold or shared with third parties.

Future Vision

CreatorHub aims to expand into a global digital education marketplace by introducing:

  • Creator analytics dashboard
  • Advanced rating & review systems
  • Trending & bestseller algorithms
  • Premium creator subscriptions
